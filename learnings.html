NFTs are allowing content creators to connect with the community and sell tokens for exclusive content and build a one of a kind art from that specific content creator. These artworks are designed so that you cannot copy or duplicate them.








Top NFTs can sell for millions of dollars. NFTs are a great example of a new technology enabling people to create and share. Collectors of NFTs say they feel a sense of pride similar to owning a one of a kind baseball card.








non-fungible token:
a : a unique digital identifier that cannot be copied, substituted, or subdivided, that is recorded in a blockchain, and that is used to certify authenticity and ownership (as of a specific digital asset and specific rights relating to it)



While we are starting to see other cryptocurrencies have their own versions of NFTs, mainstream NFTs are supported by the Ethereum Blockchain. NFTs rely on blockchain transactions to validate their authenticity and record their ownership; in most cases, you need crypto to purchase NFTs.











NFTs have fundamentally changed the market for digital assets. Before the advent of NFTs, there weren't any secure ways to confirm ownership of these types of assets. While artwork is currently the focus, NFTs can help verify the validity and ownership of anything from a vote to downloaded memories from your brain (if technology ever has the capability of downloading memories that is :-D ).
