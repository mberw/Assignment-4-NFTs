Cool Cats NFT

All time trading volume: $238 million

Cool Cats is a collection of 10,000 algorithmically generated NFTs. They initially cost 0.06 Etherium (roughly $200), and include various attributes from over 300,000 total options.

Negatives of NFTs

NFTs rely on cryptocurrencies that cause carbon emissions

Value for NFTs can be uncertain. When you own an NFT, you own a record saying you own the token behind the original asset. People can still copy and paste the asset on social media.

It's also not really the same as owning one of a kind physical art.
