Once you have some digital art, it's time to find a marketplace! One of the most well known (and free!) marketplaces for NFTs is OpenSea. This is where we will post Top Hat Man for the world to see and bid on! Who knows, perhaps he'll become as famous as some other well known and best selling NFTs!







It's time to mint our NFT! On OpenSea, we follow a simple process of adding our digital to a collection. We also add a short description of the artwork and a link we can provide so people can learn more about the art and artist. Click the create button and our artwork is minted into a shiny new NFT.






This is Top Hat Man.
He is a one of a kind digital drawing made by my oldest son specifically for the purpose of this assignment and to be made into an NFT and placed on a marketplace to be sold. I wanted to explore the process of creating an NFT from start to finish to gain a better understanding of how it all works.



















Now that we have selected our marketplace, we need an Ethereum wallet to store our Ether. Ether is the world's second largest virtual currency. There are several Ethereum wallets to choose from and I decided on MetaMask.










Now that our NFT has been minted, we need to let the world know about it! I decided to set the price for Top Hat Man at 0.025 ETH, or $43 USD. Top Hat Man may be unique, but we've never sold anything before, so lets not get ahead of ourselves! It's now time to market our NFT on social media sites and NFT forums around the web. I can see the Ether flowing now!

Do I have any bidders?!
